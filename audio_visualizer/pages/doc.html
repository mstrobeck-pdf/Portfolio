Inspiration and Idea

When it came to a final project, the only concept I knew I wanted to explore more was how to utilize an audio visualizer and creat pleasant scenes that can be customizable by the user. Since winter came around, I knew I wanted something festive but something that also made the user want to look forward to the sunny weather again. Therefore, I utilized various shapes and created unique scenes that took me hours planning out on paper and then having to figure out how to create using Canvas. This project consists of trial and error but also in some cases started from scratch as ideas constantly changed.
